About Setyo Dev

Setyo Dev is a passionate frontend developer and dedicated coding educator who believes in the transformative power of technology education. With years of experience in web development and a deep commitment to nurturing young talents, Setyo Dev has dedicated his career to making programming accessible and exciting for students of all backgrounds.

As an educator, Setyo Dev goes beyond teaching syntax and frameworks. The mission is to inspire students to discover their potential in the IT field, particularly in programming. Every lesson is crafted to ignite curiosity, build confidence, and develop problem-solving skills that extend far beyond the computer screen.

What makes Setyo Dev's approach unique is the emphasis on project-based learning with meaningful impact. Students are encouraged to create projects that promote positive values, build constructive communities, and contribute to society in meaningful ways. This philosophy ensures that technical skills are developed alongside character building and social responsibility.

Technical Skills & Expertise

With a comprehensive skill set in modern web development, Setyo Dev equips students with industry-relevant knowledge and practical experience. All these skills are systematically taught to students, enabling them to build real-world applications and pursue their interests in IT.
